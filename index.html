Technoblade

1999 - 2022

The Blood God

Alexander "Alex", known online as Technoblade, was an American YouTuber and streamer celebrated for his incredible skill in Minecraft, his deadpan humor, and his engaging storytelling. He rose to fame as one of the most dominant players on the Hypixel server and became a central figure in the Dream SMP, capturing the hearts of millions with his iconic pig-king avatar and unwavering spirit.

A Legendary Timeline

  • 2013 - Uploads his first public video and begins his journey on YouTube.
  • 2016-2018 - Gains widespread recognition for his "Bedwars" and "Skywars" series, establishing his dominance on the Hypixel server.
  • 2019 - Wins Minecraft Monday, a weekly tournament hosted by Keemstar, multiple times, solidifying his reputation as a top-tier PvP player.
  • 2020 - Engages in the "Great Potato War," a friendly but intense rivalry with fellow YouTuber Im_a_squid_kid to farm the most potatoes in Hypixel Skyblock, a feat of legendary dedication.
  • 2020 - Joins the Dream SMP, where his character—an anarchist who opposed all forms of government—became a fan-favorite and a pivotal part of the server's lore.
  • August 2021 - Announces his diagnosis with sarcoma, a rare form of cancer, facing the news with his characteristic humor and resolve.
  • June 2022 - In a final video titled "so long nerds," his father reads a posthumous message, revealing that Alex had passed away. The video served as a final, heartfelt goodbye to his community.

Legacy and Impact

Technoblade's legacy extends far beyond his gaming achievements. He inspired a generation of gamers with his perseverance, wit, and the philosophy that with enough dedication, you could achieve anything—even farming 500 million potatoes. His famous line, "Technoblade never dies," transformed from a meme into a mantra for his community, symbolizing his lasting impact on the lives of his fans and friends. He will be remembered not only as a great entertainer but as a kind soul who brought joy and laughter to millions.
